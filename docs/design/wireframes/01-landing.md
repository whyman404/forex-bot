## Design notes

- Hero CTA "Start Free Trial" is primary — no credit card copy near CTA
- Risk disclaimer appears in hero AND footer — not buried
- Strategy performance numbers are exact from backtest, not rounded up
- Pricing page does not show "win rate" prominently — show PF + Drawdown first (Priya's mental model)
- Mobile nav: hamburger opens full-screen overlay, not sidebar (less cognitive load)
